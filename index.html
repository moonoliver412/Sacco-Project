Who We Are

A cooperative rooted in faith, built for community.

Founded in 2015 by members of PCEA Dandora Church, Jiunge Upate SACCO is registered under the Cooperative Societies Act No. 12 of 1997 and governed by the Cooperative Societies Act of 2008 and our bylaws.

We exist to promote a saving and investment culture for the sustainable economic and social enhancement of our members and the wider community we belong to.

Vision

A model SACCO

Empowering the community economically, socially, and spiritually.

Mission

Sustainable savings

Promoting an investment culture for lasting member prosperity.
