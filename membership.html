Three pathways. One community.

Whether you're joining as an individual, registering your business, or bringing in a self-help group — here's exactly what you'll need.

— Choose your path

How to join Jiunge Upate.

Pick the membership type that fits you. Each path has its own requirements, but the welcome is always the same.

Join as an individual.

Individual membership is the most common pathway into Jiunge Upate. It's how most members start — saving steadily, accessing fair credit when needed, and growing together with the cooperative.

Once your registration is processed, you'll receive your membership number, access to all loan products (subject to savings tenure), and the ability to contribute to your share capital.

What you'll need

  1. Duly completed registration form
  2. 2 passport-size photographs
  3. Copy of your National ID
  4. Registration fee payment (non-refundable)
  5. ID processing fee payment
  6. Initial share capital contribution

Fees & minimums

Registration fee (non-refundable) KES 1,000
ID processing fee KES 100
Minimum share capital (500 shares @ KES 20) KES 10,000
Minimum monthly contribution KES 500

Join as a corporate.

Corporate membership is designed for registered businesses, organizations and institutions seeking a cooperative home for institutional savings and access to commercial-scale lending.

Once your business is registered, your nominated office bearers can transact on behalf of the entity in line with the resolution submitted at onboarding.

What you'll need

  1. Completed registration form
  2. 2 passport photos of each office bearer
  3. Business registration certificate
  4. Business PIN certificate
  5. Board resolution authorizing account opening

Fees & minimums

Registration fee (non-refundable) KES 3,000
Minimum share capital KES 10,000
Minimum monthly contribution KES 2,000

Join as a self-help group.

Self-help groups, chamas, and community collectives are warmly welcomed at Jiunge Upate. Pooling resources is at the very heart of the cooperative model — and we're built for it.

Bring along your group documentation, and our team will walk through how the SACCO can support your savings and lending objectives.

What you'll need

  1. List of all members
  2. Telephone numbers for each member
  3. National ID numbers for each member

Good to know

Onboarding type Custom
Eligibility Open

Visit us in person to set up your group's arrangement — we'll customize terms with your group leadership based on size, purpose, and contribution model.

— The process

From form to first deposit.

A simple, four-step path from your first visit to becoming a fully active member.

— step 01

Visit & collect form

Stop by our office at PCEA Dandora Church, or call us to receive your registration form digitally.

— step 02

Submit your documents

Complete the form, gather your supporting documents, and pay the applicable registration fees.

— step 03

Begin contributing

Make your first share capital contribution and start your monthly savings discipline.

— step 04

Welcome to the cooperative

From your first deposit, you're a full member with access to all our products, programs, and the community of fellow members building toward shared prosperity.
