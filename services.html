— 04 / Policy

Withdrawal terms, in plain words.

We believe in clarity over fine print. Here is exactly how withdrawals work at Jiunge Upate.

Shares

Shares are not refundable, but they are transferable. If you choose to leave the SACCO, your share capital can be transferred to another eligible member.

Deposits

Deposits are refundable after a 60-day account review period. This window allows our cooperative to reconcile any outstanding obligations and ensures fair treatment of every member.
